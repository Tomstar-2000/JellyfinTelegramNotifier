Telegram Notifier

Sends Telegram sendPhoto messages when new movies, episodes or seasons are added to your Jellyfin library.

Telegram Settings

Your Telegram bot token from @BotFather.
Target chat/channel ID. Use @username or numeric ID.
The public-facing URL of your Jellyfin server. Required for images.

Message Templates

Available tokens: {Title} {Year} {Overview} {SeriesName} {SeasonNumber} {EpisodeNumber} {EpisodeTitle} {AddedDate} {Rating} {Genres}

Season Completion Logic

Percentage of a season's total episodes that must be present before a season-level notification is sent. At 100% all episodes must be present. Below this threshold, individual episode notifications are sent instead.

TVDB Integration (Optional)

When enabled, TVDB is queried to get the true episode count for a season before deciding whether to send a season-level notification.
Obtain from thetvdb.com/api-information.

Advanced

How many past notification records to keep in the Retrigger panel below.
Wait this many minutes after a library addition before sending. If a Library Scan is running, notifications will wait until the scan completes.

Recent Events

Review past notifications. You can edit the caption and retrigger any entry, or delete it so the item will be re-notified on the next library scan.
